Wipe All Argus Data?

This will permanently delete all your data:

  • Projects, bookmarks, monitors, feeds
  • Analysis history & knowledge graph
  • All settings (API keys included)
  • OPFS snapshots & cached results

This action cannot be undone. Consider downloading a backup first.

After wiping, the Firefox extension manager will open so you can remove Argus completely.

## Description

**Requires your own API key - cheap, easy, full control. No subscriptions to Argus.**

Argus connects directly to AI providers using your own API keys. Most offer free tiers or credits to get started - a typical page analysis costs less than a penny. Compare that to $20/month ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro. Run Ollama locally and it costs nothing.

Get a key in ~2 minutes:
- Grok (xAI): console.x.ai - $25/month free API credits
- Gemini (Google): aistudio.google.com - generous free tier
- OpenAI: platform.openai.com - pay-as-you-go
- Claude (Anthropic): console.anthropic.com - $5 free credit on signup
- Custom: any OpenAI-compatible endpoint (Ollama, LM Studio, Hugging Face, self-hosted)

Your keys stay on your machine - no Argus servers, no telemetry, no accounts.
